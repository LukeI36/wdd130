Welcome to Rapids Whitewater Rafting

Let us introduce ourselves

Who are we?

We are Rapids Whitewater Rafting, a water rafting company that has been in service since 1968. Our purpose here is to help organize and prepare rafting trips near our area (Arthurs Point, New Zealand) for customers who booked/registered online.

What do we offer?

We have 5 different rafting trips that customers can experience for themselves or with others. Each rafting trip has an intensity level, so customers can choose whether their experience here is intense, calm, or adventurous. Whatever trip it is, we are sure that everyone gets the necessary equipment and that one of our staff acts as a guide/leader during the trip to make sure everyone stays safe.
